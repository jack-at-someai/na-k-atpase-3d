# Serengeti Prides — Session Frameworks

## Session Structure (90 minutes)

Every Pride meeting follows this flow:

| Segment | Duration | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Check-In** | 10 min | Personal/professional update from each member |
| **Hot Seat** | 40 min | One member presents a challenge; group works it |
| **Skill Module** | 25 min | Structured learning on a leadership topic |
| **Commitments** | 10 min | Each member states one action item for next session |
| **Close** | 5 min | Gratitude round and meeting eval |

## Hot Seat Protocol

The Hot Seat is the heart of every Pride meeting. One member gets the floor.

### Rules
1. **Presenter** has 10 minutes to describe their challenge (no interruptions)
2. **Clarifying questions** from the group (5 minutes)
3. **Group discussion** — advice, experience, frameworks (20 minutes)
4. **Presenter summarizes** what they'll do next (5 minutes)
5. **Confidentiality** — what happens in the Hot Seat stays in the Hot Seat

### Rotation
Every member gets the Hot Seat at least twice per quarter. Track with a simple rotation schedule.
