## Research Tracks

Singularity is the R&D arm. Current areas of exploration:

### 1. AI-Augmented Deal Sourcing
Use Charlotte's signal processing + ML models from the nature-inspired-algorithms research to scan for dying apps that match Farms criteria. Automated scoring, market sizing, and competitive analysis.

### 2. Decentralized Ownership Models
Explore tokenized equity or blockchain-based ownership for Farms portfolio companies. Lower the barrier for Pride members to earn ownership. Informed by swarm intelligence research on distributed decision-making.

### 3. Serengeti Talent Network
Build an internal talent marketplace where Pride members can be deployed to Farms companies. Charlotte tracks member skills as signals; matching is graph traversal. Skills matching, project-based work, equity compensation.

### 4. Cross-Portfolio Intelligence
Charlotte's domain-agnostic substrate enables pattern detection across verticals. Signals from Sounder (biological lifecycles) can inform ServiceIQ (mechanical lifecycles). This cross-pollination is unique to the Charlotte architecture.

### 5. Charlotte Licensing
Once Charlotte proves across 3+ verticals, license the substrate itself as "Observable Reality as a Service." Competitors: Neo4j, Amazon Neptune, Power BI — but none have time-native signals + protocols.

### 6. Serengeti Media & Content
Build a media arm (podcast, newsletter, social) that amplifies the Serengeti brand, attracts talent, and creates a top-of-funnel for both Founders recruitment and Pride enrollment.

### 7. International Expansion
Replicate the Serengeti model in other markets. Serengeti EU, Serengeti LATAM, Serengeti APAC. Each with local Founders and Prides, feeding into a global Farms pipeline with Charlotte as the shared substrate.
