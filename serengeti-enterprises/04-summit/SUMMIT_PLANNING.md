# Serengeti Summit — Event Planning Guide

> *"Climb the Mountain so we can see the world. Not so the world can see you."*

## What Is Summit?

The annual Serengeti Summit is a multi-day event that brings together Founders, Pride members, mentors, guest speakers, and the broader Serengeti ecosystem for networking, learning, and strategic alignment.

## Attendee Profile

- Serengeti Founders (all 12)
- Pride members (all active cohorts)
- Guest speakers and thought leaders
- Selected guests and prospective members
- Serengeti operations team

### Who Thrives at Summit?
- **Learners** — Committed to personal development, always improving
- **Growth Oriented** — Discontent with status quo, looking for better ways
- **Confident, Yet Humble** — Willing to help others, but still coachable
- **Contributors** — Willing to participate in the overall growth of the Pride

## Event Framework

### Day 1: Connect
| Time | Session | Format |
|---|---|---|
| AM | Welcome & State of Serengeti | Keynote (Founders) |
| AM | Icebreaker: Pride Dynamics | Small group activity |
| PM | Industry Panels | 3 concurrent tracks |
| PM | Networking Reception | Open format |
| Eve | Founders Dinner (Founders only) | Private |

### Day 2: Learn
| Time | Session | Format |
|---|---|---|
| AM | Keynote Speaker | External thought leader |
| AM | Breakout: Skills Workshops | 4 concurrent tracks |
| PM | Serengeti Farms Demo Day | Portfolio companies present |
| PM | Mentor Speed Rounds | 1-on-1 mentor matching |
| Eve | Group Dinner | All attendees |

### Day 3: Build
| Time | Session | Format |
|---|---|---|
| AM | Innovation Sprint | Teams solve a real Farms challenge |
| AM | Pitch Competition | Teams present solutions |
| PM | Closing Keynote & Awards | Celebration |
| PM | Departure | — |

## Planning Timeline

| Months Out | Task |
|---|---|
| 12 | Set date, begin venue scouting |
| 9 | Confirm venue, begin speaker outreach |
| 6 | Open registration, confirm speakers, plan logistics |
| 4 | Finalize agenda, book travel for speakers |
| 3 | Marketing push, attendee communications |
| 2 | Print materials, confirm catering, A/V setup |
| 1 | Final attendee list, name badges, run sheets |
| 1 week | On-site walkthrough, tech rehearsal |
| Day of | Execute |
| +1 week | Post-event survey, thank-you notes |
| +1 month | Impact report to Founders |

## Success Metrics

- [ ] Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 70
- [ ] 90%+ attendance rate from confirmed registrants
- [ ] 3+ actionable connections reported per attendee
- [ ] At least 1 new Farms idea generated from Innovation Sprint
- [ ] Post-event engagement increase in Prides (measured by session attendance)
