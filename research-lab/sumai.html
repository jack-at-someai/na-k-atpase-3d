The Lab
The OASIS

Designed using Central Place Theory — hexagonal tessellation that maximizes accessibility, minimizes travel distance, and creates natural hierarchies of specialization.

The Structure

SomeAI studies the individual human senses — how they work, how they can be mimicked, how they can be augmented by AI, and what happens when you combine them.

The Path
The Progression System

Every researcher enters the OASIS at Level I of their primary sense. As they deepen, they gain clearance to adjacent senses — where cross-sensory research happens.

I
Fundamentals
Sensing, signal acquisition, basic AI models. The entry point for every researcher.
Years 1-2
II
Advanced
Real-time processing, domain-specific applications. First cross-sensory clearance unlocked.
Years 2-3
III
Expert
Novel research, pushing the frontier of that sense. Two-sense combinations, primary to Level IV.
Years 3-5
IV
Pioneer
Contributions that define the field. Three-sense combinations, mentoring new Level I researchers.
Years 5-7
V
Architect
Designing the infrastructure that future researchers build on. Four/five-sense integration or deep specialization.
Years 7-8
The Combinations
Cross-Sensory Research

The boundaries between hex sections are where cross-sensory research happens. The combinations themselves reveal new disciplines.

Surgical Robotics
Haptic feedback from visual input. The surgeon sees and feels through the machine simultaneously.
Audio-Visual Scene Understanding
Lip reading, multimodal event detection, audio-visual source separation. What you see confirms what you hear.
Food Science
Texture-flavor correlation, mouthfeel engineering. The crunch of a chip is touch, the salt is taste — inseparable.
Molecular Gastronomy
Pharmaceutical formulation, flavor engineering at the molecular level. 80% of what we call "taste" is actually smell.
Environmental Monitoring
Gas leak detection via acoustic + chemical signatures. Industrial safety through multi-modal sensing.
Full Culinary Stack
The Enigma connection. Every plate at a 3-Michelin-star restaurant is a five-sense signal. This is the science behind atmosphere.
Autonomous Manipulation
Robots that see, feel, and hear their environment. Sensory feedback loops that mirror biological systems.
Full Multimodal AI
Charlotte observing reality through all channels simultaneously. The innermost ring around Brain. Years of combined research to unlock.
