Company History

1990 — Roland Schumaker launches The Show Circuit Magazine, building a livestock media brand that would reach 50,000+ readers across species and become the editorial backbone of the operation.

2015 — SC Online Sales launches as a multi-species online auction platform out of Robinson, Illinois. From day one, the platform covers cattle, sheep, goats, pigs, and equine — a breadth no competitor matches.

2018 — ShowStockPlanet launches as a secondary sale platform. SC2Day blog begins publishing digital content and sale previews. The media ecosystem expands.

2020 — COVID-19 accelerates online adoption. SC Online Sales launches a virtual show platform for 4-H and FFA exhibitors, keeping youth programs running when in-person events shut down.

2024 — Platform surpasses 40,000 auctions conducted. Field rep team expands to four. Instagram reaches 23,000 followers. Self-service sale booking goes live.

2026 — SC Online Sales operates as the leading multi-species online livestock auction platform. Five species, four platforms, three decades of media trust.
