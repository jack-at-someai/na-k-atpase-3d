Brand Essence

The invisible infrastructure company

SomeAI builds operating systems for operations. The logo is a spider web — nature's most efficient knowledge graph. Every strand is a connection, every node is an anchor, and the web itself is invisible until it catches the light. Like Charlotte's substrate, the best infrastructure disappears into the thing it serves.

Mission
Build invisible infrastructure for the 33.2 million businesses technology forgot. The correct measure of progress is not how much technology people use — it is how little they notice.
Voice
Precise, confident, unhurried. We do not explain ourselves defensively. We state what we build and why it matters. Technical depth without jargon. The tone of someone who has done the work.
Metaphor
Spider silk. Strongest natural material on Earth. Invisible at scale. A web is simultaneously a structure, a sensor network, a home, and a trap — all from one material, one builder, one design.
Promise
Every business gets its own operating system — compiled from its own signals, shrunk to its own instruction set — without knowing it exists. The adoption tax disappears because there is nothing to adopt.

Logo — The Web

The mark is a spider web. It reads simultaneously as a knowledge graph (nodes and edges), a convex hull (the outer boundary), and a web of connections (the business substrate). Nine radial threads converge at a center node. Three concentric rings hold the structure together. The form is organic, slightly irregular — like a real web, like a real business.

Usage Rules

Do

Use the logo mark at full opacity on dark, light, or red backgrounds. Maintain clear space equal to the inner ring radius on all sides.

Do

Use the horizontal lockup for primary placements. The mark goes left, the wordmark goes right. Never stack them vertically.

Don't

Never rotate, skew, add drop shadows, or apply effects to the logo. The web's geometry is precise. Distortion breaks the metaphor.

Don't

Never use colors outside the palette on the logo. The red spectrum is the brand. Blue, green, or rainbow treatments are not SomeAI.

Do

Use SomeAI Red 500 (#E53E3E) as the primary action color. Red 600 for hover states. Red 400 for highlights on dark backgrounds.

Don't

Never use the logo mark smaller than 24px. The web's inner details become illegible. At small sizes, simplify to the outer ring only.
