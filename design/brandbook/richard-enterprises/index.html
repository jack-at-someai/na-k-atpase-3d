Strategy — private equity holding company and three-generation family office

Richard Enterprises is the capital allocation spine of the Richard family — bridging industrial operations, frontier R&D, and multi-generational wealth stewardship. The Wayne + Stark DNA encoded across every subsidiary and initiative.

Mission
Small-to-middle market acquisitions ($5–50M EBITDA). Capital allocation + industrial operations + frontier R&D. The Wayne + Stark DNA.
Structure
Three subsidiaries: ISG (industrial operations, 80K employees), Serengeti Enterprises (farms, prides, summit, oasis), SomeAI Research Lab (Charlotte substrate).
Philosophy
Wayne side = capital networks, institutional patience, individual investor focus. Stark side = Charlotte graph-native infrastructure, 37 research instruments, academic pedigree.
Principals
Jim Richard (Chairman, Capital), Joseph Richard (Operations), Jack Richard (R&D/Technology, Northwestern MSAI, Charlotte creator), Dave Genger (The Origin, intellectual trajectory).
