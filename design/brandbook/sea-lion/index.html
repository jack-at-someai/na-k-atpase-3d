Brand Essence

Ocean science research for the rescue diver building a dive shop with umbrella brand

Sea Lion brings Charlotte's knowledge graph to marine operations — tracking dive training, reef ecosystems, water quality, sensor data, and monitoring protocols across ocean environments. The platform observes what divers and marine researchers already do and makes it legible.

Domain
Ocean science research. Dive training, marine biology, reef monitoring, underwater survey work. The marine instruction set compiled from Charlotte's kernel.
Palette Logic
Ocean blue for depth and exploration. Amber for discovery and warmth. Cyan for research precision. Green for ecosystem health.
Logo
Lion wearing dive mask and snorkel — literal "sea lion". Assets in multiple formats (PDF concepts, PNG, JPG mockups).
Charlotte Layer
Marine ecosystem as knowledge graph. Reef systems as nodes, currents as edges, water quality as metrics, sensor readings as signals, monitoring protocols as protocols.

Product

🤿

Dive Training

Rescue diver pathway programs. Certification tracking, skill progression, and safety protocols.

🔬

Marine Research

Partnerships and data collection. Collaborative science with universities and conservation groups.

🐚

Reef Monitoring

Underwater data and ecosystem tracking. Coral health, species counts, and environmental metrics.

⚙

Equipment Lab

Gear evaluation and reviews. Testing protocols, maintenance schedules, and performance benchmarks.

📊

Survey Methods

Underwater survey methodology. Transect design, sampling frameworks, and data analysis pipelines.

🏪

Dive Shop

Retail + umbrella brand operations. Equipment sales, trip booking, and community hub.
