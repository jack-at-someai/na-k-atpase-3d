Company History

1975 — Earl Ramsey opens All State Manufacturing as a vending machine remanufacturer in Terre Haute, Indiana.

~2000s — Marian Ramsey Ford (Earl's daughter) transitions from remanufacturing to specialty shelving and cabinetry for the vending industry.

2017 — Rudolph Stakeman Jr. purchases the company and all assets from Marian Ramsey Ford.

2018 — New 180-foot powder coating line installed — Midwest Finishing Systems conveyor with inline curing furnace. Manufacturing cycle cut from 3 days to same-day.

2025 — 50 years in business. A+ BBB rating. Zero complaints. NAMA Legacy Exhibitor. Ready for digital transformation.
