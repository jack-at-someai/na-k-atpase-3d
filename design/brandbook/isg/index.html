Brand Essence

Making our legacies our future

ISG is a powerhouse of expert industrial service and distribution brands. Each brand brings decades of specialized legacy. United under ISG, they create a one-stop-shop experience for critical-to-process rotating equipment — without sacrificing the deep expertise that made each brand a leader.

Positioning
One-stop-shop for rotating equipment. Maximize uptime. Simplify procurement. Work with a network of dedicated experts. Each brand keeps its legacy identity while gaining network scale.
Model
Acquisition-driven family of companies. Each brand is a specialized legacy in its market — valves, compressors, motors, pumps. ISG provides the umbrella, shared infrastructure, and cross-sell network.
Voice
Technical authority without jargon. The tone of people who work with their hands and know their equipment cold. Direct, reliable, no-nonsense. We fix it, we keep it running, we have the part.
