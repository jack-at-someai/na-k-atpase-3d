Brand Essence

Biological lifecycle intelligence for the show pig industry

Sounder brings Charlotte's knowledge graph to biological operations — tracking genetics, gestation, health, lineage, and performance across generations. The platform observes what breeders already do and makes it legible.

Domain
Show pig herd management. Health tracking, breeding cycles, lineage records, performance metrics, sales tracking. The biological instruction set compiled from Charlotte's kernel.
Palette Logic
Purple for intelligence and trust. Magenta for vitality and biological energy. Cyan for clarity and data. Red for alerts and health. Four 9-step palettes provide depth across any context.
Entity
Sounder Enterprises, LLC (Delaware). Product: SounderApp.com. MVP complete, beta in progress. Charlotte's first biological domain validation.
Charlotte Layer
Sounder is a compiled Charlotte OS — the kernel's five primitives (Node, Edge, Metric, Signal, Protocol) mapped to sows, litters, gestation timelines, health signals, and breeding protocols.

Product

🧬

Lineage & Genetics

Full pedigree tracking. Sire-dam-litter relationships as EDGE primitives in the graph.

📈

Breeding Cycles

Gestation tracking, heat detection, breeding schedules. Temporal spine at the biological scale.

❤

Health Tracking

Per-sow health history. Signals on metric lines. Threshold alerts when vitals cross boundaries.

🏆

Show Performance

Exhibition results, judge feedback, competitive records. The heuristic layer of the domain.

💰

Sales Tracking

Pig sales, buyer records, revenue metrics. Business signals flowing into the graph.

🔔

Push Notifications

Breeding windows, health events, due dates. Protocols firing on schedule.
