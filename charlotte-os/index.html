Charlotte OS is a boot image written entirely in first-order logic. It defines five irreducible primitives, a temporal spine from Millennium to Hour, a spatial substrate across all 50 US states, and five types of knowledge. Every domain OS — from swine genetics to equipment intelligence — compiles on top of this kernel. The boot sequence loads 31 KRF files across 6 phases to bring a container to operational state.
