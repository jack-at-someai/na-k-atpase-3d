Core Features

LineSkips

Purchase a pass to skip the line at participating bars and venues. Dynamic pricing adjusts based on real-time demand so you always get a fair deal.

Cover

Pay cover charges directly from your phone. No cash, no ATM runs, no hassle. Just scan the QR code at the door and walk in.

Event Tickets

Browse and purchase tickets to concerts, themed nights, and special events at bars and venues near you. All in one app.

Venue Dashboard

A comprehensive suite of tools built for bar owners, managers, and promoters.

  • Real-time sales analytics
  • Customer CRM & segmentation
  • Push notification campaigns
  • SMS & email marketing
  • Dynamic pricing engine
  • Revenue reporting & exports
  • Multi-venue management
  • Staff access controls

Event Ticketing Platform

Everything you need to sell out your next event, built right into the LineLeap ecosystem.

  • In-app event promotion
  • Promo codes & discounts
  • Tiered pricing (Early Bird, GA, VIP)
  • Guest list management
  • QR code scanning at door
  • Real-time ticket sales tracking
  • Capacity management tools
  • Bundled ticket + drink packages
