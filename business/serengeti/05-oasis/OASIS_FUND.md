# Serengeti Oasis — Private Equity Real Estate Fund

> *"An oasis nurtures the heart, the mind and the soul while providing the will to move forward."*

## What Is Oasis?

Serengeti Oasis is a private equity fund for Serengeti Founders and select members to collectively own and enjoy a portfolio of exceptional luxury residences in top destinations — while benefiting from the portfolio's appreciation over the fund's lifetime.

## Fund Structure

### The Residences
- Homes typically valued between **$1.5M and $3.0M**
- Located in desirable **beach, mountain, and city destinations**
- Investors can reserve any home in the fund anytime online

### Investment Tiers

| Tier | Annual Nights | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 7 nights | Entry-level access |
| Tier 2 | 14 nights | Mid-tier, most popular |
| Tier 3 | 28 nights | Full access, priority booking |

### Economics
- **Initial contribution**: Based on tier selected
- **Annual dues**: Cover shared operating costs (maintenance, housekeeping, management)
- **At wind-down**: Homes are sold, net proceeds distributed:
  - First: 100% of original investment returned
  - Then: 80% of remaining appreciation to investors

### Availability
- Target **40% average vacancy rate** — intentionally underutilized for exceptional access
- Members book on their own schedule
