## Phase Gates

The flywheel doesn't go from zero to singularity overnight. There are discrete phases with measurable gates:

### Phase 1: Ignition (Current)
**Objective**: Prove the model works with Farms #1.

| Gate | Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Capital raised | Farms #1 subscriptions | 6+ of 12 units committed |
| Apps launched | Portfolio companies live | 2 apps in market |
| Revenue | Monthly recurring | $10K+ MRR across portfolio |
| Prides | First cohort running | 1 cohort, 6+ members |
| Charlotte | Substrate validated | Sounder + ServiceIQ live on Charlotte |

**Unlock**: Proof that Founders invest → Farms builds → Revenue returns.

### Phase 2: Traction (Year 1–2)
**Objective**: Multiple revenue streams and talent pipeline active.

| Gate | Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Capital returned | Distributions to Farms #1 investors | First distributions paid |
| Farms #2 | Second tranche raised | 10+ units committed |
| Portfolio revenue | Monthly across all apps | $100K+ MRR |
| Pride ideas | Ideas that became Farms projects | 2+ ideas sourced from Prides |
| Summit | First Summit executed | 50+ attendees, NPS > 70 |

**Unlock**: Multi-tranche operation, talent-to-product pipeline proven.

### Phase 3: Momentum (Year 2–4)
**Objective**: System generates more than it consumes.

| Gate | Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Self-funding | Farms revenue > new tranche cost | Revenue-funded tranches |
| Founder waitlist | Qualified applicants > available seats | 2x oversubscribed |
| Oasis | First properties acquired | 3+ properties, positive cash flow |
| Charlotte | 3+ verticals on substrate | Swine, nightlife, industrial + 1 more |
| Media | Serengeti brand as top-of-funnel | Podcast/newsletter with 10K+ audience |

**Unlock**: Flywheel is self-sustaining. External capital is additive, not required.

### Phase 4: Singularity (Year 4+)
**Objective**: Exponential, irreversible growth.

| Gate | Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Portfolio exits | M&A or licensing events | 2+ exits at 5x+ return |
| International | Serengeti operating in 2+ markets | EU or LATAM Pride + Farms |
| Charlotte licensing | Substrate licensed to external parties | Revenue from Charlotte itself |
| Team | Operations run without founder daily involvement | Full management team |
| Ecosystem | All 6 divisions profitable independently | Zero subsidy between divisions |

## Energy Accounting

Think of each division as producing and consuming energy:

| Division | Produces | Consumes |
|---|---|---|
| **Founders** | Capital, network, wisdom, governance | Time, attention, trust |
| **Farms** | Revenue, IP, portfolio companies | Capital, dev resources, Charlotte capacity |
| **Prides** | Talent, ideas, future Founders | Curriculum, mentors, admin |
| **Summit** | Network, brand, recruitment pipeline | Event costs, logistics, speaker fees |
| **Oasis** | Passive income, lifestyle, retention | Property acquisition capital, management |
| **Charlotte** | Shared infrastructure, speed, intelligence | R&D investment, maintenance |

**Singularity = Total energy produced > Total energy consumed, sustained indefinitely.**

## The Charlotte Multiplier

Charlotte is the hidden accelerant in the flywheel. Without Charlotte:
- Each Farms app is a standalone build (6+ months, $200K+)
- No cross-portfolio intelligence
- No shared infrastructure cost savings
- Linear scaling only

With Charlotte:
- Each Farms app is a new vertical on existing substrate (2–3 months, $50K–$80K)
- Cross-portfolio pattern detection (signals from Sounder inform ServiceIQ protocols)
- Infrastructure investment amortized across every portfolio company
- **Exponential scaling** — each new app makes the substrate more valuable

This is why Charlotte isn't just a tool — it's the **compound interest engine** of the entire Serengeti ecosystem.
