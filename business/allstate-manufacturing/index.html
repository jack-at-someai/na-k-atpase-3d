Company Profile

Three generations of American metal

1975
Earl Ramsey opens All State Manufacturing as a vending machine remanufacturer in Terre Haute, Indiana.
~2000s
Marian Ramsey Ford (Earl's daughter) transitions the business from remanufacturing to specialty shelving and cabinetry for the vending industry.
2017
Rudolph "Rudy" Stakeman Jr. purchases the company and all assets. Brings experience from Specialty Blanks manufacturing.
2018
New 180-foot powder coating line installed — Midwest Finishing Systems conveyor with inline curing furnace. Cuts manufacturing cycle from 3 days to same-day assembly.
2025
Today — 50 years in business, A+ BBB rating, NAMA Legacy Exhibitor, zero complaints. Ready for digital infrastructure.
