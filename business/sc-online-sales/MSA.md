# Sounder — Strategic Partnership Agreement
## Sounder × Show Circuit Online Sales, LLC

**STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT**

This Strategic Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **April 1, 2026** ("Effective Date") by and between:

**Sounder**, a division of SomeAI Research Lab / Richard Enterprises ("Technology Partner"), with principal offices at Paris, Illinois

and

**Show Circuit Online Sales, LLC** ("Industry Partner"), with principal offices at 1730 W. Main Street, Robinson, IL 62454

collectively referred to as the "Parties."

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Charlotte OS"** means the Technology Partner's proprietary operational intelligence substrate, including all graph infrastructure, signal processing pipelines, protocol engines, and derived analytics capabilities.

**1.2 "Substrate"** means the deployed instance of Charlotte OS configured for the Industry Partner's multi-species livestock auction domain, including all nodes, edges, metrics, signals, and protocols spanning seller operations, buyer behavior, sale management, and cross-species analytics.

**1.3 "Signal History"** means the append-only record of all observations, measurements, and events ingested into the Substrate over time, including auction data, bidder behavior, seller activity, settlement records, and species-level performance.

**1.4 "Protocols"** means the decision frameworks, expected trajectories, drift thresholds, and evaluation criteria configured within the Substrate to generate operational intelligence.

**1.5 "Deliverables"** means the analytics reports, platform enhancements, drift alerts, trajectory analyses, digital assets, and operational outputs produced by the Technology Partner.

**1.6 "Domain Data"** means the raw operational records, auction data, sale catalogs, bidder records, seller information, lot data, species records, and historical observations provided by the Industry Partner for ingestion into the Substrate.

**1.7 "Platform"** means SC Online Sales (sconlinesales.com), ShowStockPlanet (showstockplanet.com), and associated digital properties operated by the Industry Partner.

## 2. SCOPE OF PARTNERSHIP

**2.1 Partnership Model.** This is a strategic technology partnership. The Technology Partner provides Charlotte OS infrastructure to modernize and extend the Industry Partner's auction platforms. The Industry Partner provides multi-species auction data, seller/buyer networks, and distribution channels through The Show Circuit Magazine. Both Parties benefit from the resulting platform capabilities.

**2.2 Phase 1 — Foundation (Months 1–6).** During the Foundation Phase, the Technology Partner will deploy:

- (a) **Brand identity and digital presence** — Consolidated visual identity, design system, and modernized web presence for SC Online Sales
- (b) **Domain discovery and operational walkthrough** — On-site visit to Robinson, IL headquarters and observation of the platform's sale management operations across all species divisions
- (c) **Knowledge graph seeding** — Initial Substrate deployment with entity nodes (sellers, buyers, animals, lots, sales, field reps, species) and relationship edges from historical auction data
- (d) **Auction data pipeline** — Ingestion of historical sale records into Charlotte KRF format, structured by species, seller, lot, price, date, buyer, and bidder behavior
- (e) **Seller CRM** — Unified seller graph spanning all species divisions. Every seller modeled as a NODE with sale history, species coverage, pricing patterns, and lifetime metrics
- (f) **Buyer behavior baseline** — Initial mapping of bidder patterns: cross-species purchasing, bid escalation behavior, watch list engagement, and settlement reliability
- (g) **Platform audit** — Technical assessment of the AxiaTP-built custom web application for Charlotte integration readiness
- (h) **Competitive intelligence baseline** — Mapping competitive landscape: ShowPig.com, CCI.Live, DVAuction, SaleRing.Live, StockShowAuctions.com

**2.3 Phase 2 — Operations (Months 7–24).** Following the Foundation Phase, the Technology Partner will expand to:

- (a) **Cross-species seller intelligence** — Unified analytics for sellers who operate across cattle, sheep, goats, and pigs. Combined sale performance, buyer overlap, optimal timing by species and season
- (b) **Buyer matching engine** — Predictive bidder targeting based on historical behavior, species preferences, price sensitivity, and geographic proximity
- (c) **Sale performance dashboard** — Per-sale analytics: lot count, bidder count, sale average, clearance rate, extended bidding patterns, settlement speed
- (d) **Division analytics** — Comparative performance across cattle, sheep/goat, and swine divisions. Rep performance, regional trends, species-level growth trajectories
- (e) **Show Circuit integration** — Charlotte connects magazine readership data to auction behavior, enabling targeted marketing through the publication's 50,000+ audience
- (f) **Weekly analytics delivery** — Sale performance, seller activity, bidder trends, and species-level market intelligence
- (g) **Protocol management** — Sale booking workflows, lot submission protocols, bidder verification, extended bidding rules, payment collection, consignor settlement, Pork Checkoff compliance
- (h) **Drift alerts** — Notification when monitored metrics deviate from expected trajectories: sale average decline, bidder drop-off, settlement delays, species-shift patterns
- (i) **Field rep intelligence** — Per-rep analytics: sales booked, lots managed, seller acquisition, regional coverage, close rates
- (j) **Quarterly strategic review** — Partnership review with Industry Partner leadership, including platform growth, competitive positioning, and species expansion planning

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

**3.1 Foundation Phase Fee.** During months 1 through 6 of this Agreement (April 1, 2026 through September 30, 2026), the Industry Partner shall pay a monthly partnership fee of **$3,000.00** (Three Thousand Dollars).

**3.2 Operations Phase Fee.** During months 7 through 24 of this Agreement (October 1, 2026 through March 31, 2028), the Industry Partner shall pay a monthly partnership fee of **$6,500.00** (Six Thousand Five Hundred Dollars).

**3.3 No Onboarding Fee.** The phased pricing structure incorporates onboarding into the Foundation Phase. No separate onboarding fee applies.

**3.4 Total Contract Value.** The total value of this Agreement over the full 24-month term is **$135,000.00** (One Hundred Thirty-Five Thousand Dollars), calculated as follows:

| Phase | Duration | Monthly Fee | Subtotal |
|-------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Foundation (Months 1–6) | 6 months | $3,000 | $18,000 |
| Operations (Months 7–24) | 18 months | $6,500 | $117,000 |
| **Total** | **24 months** | | **$135,000** |

**3.5 Revenue Sharing.** In addition to the monthly partnership fee, the Parties agree to the following revenue sharing arrangement for new capabilities enabled by the Substrate:

- (a) **Premium Analytics Products:** If the Parties jointly offer seller analytics, buyer intelligence, or cross-species market reports as premium products, net revenue shall be split **50% / 50%**
- (b) **Show Circuit Data Products:** If magazine readership-to-auction attribution generates a commercially viable product, net revenue shall be split **40% Technology Partner / 60% Industry Partner** (reflecting the Industry Partner's media asset)
- (c) **Cross-Platform Intelligence:** If the Technology Partner aggregates SCO data with data from other livestock platform partnerships to create industry-level intelligence products, the Industry Partner shall receive **10% of net revenue** from products incorporating their Domain Data

**3.6 Payment Terms.** All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Invoices are issued on the first business day of each month. Late payments accrue interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law.

**3.7 Taxes.** All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. The Industry Partner is responsible for all sales, use, and similar taxes arising from this Agreement.

## 4. TERM AND RENEWAL

**4.1 Initial Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date (April 1, 2026) and continue for an initial term of **twenty-four (24) months**, expiring on **March 31, 2028** (the "Initial Term").

**4.2 Renewal.** Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods at the Operations Phase Fee of $6,500/month (each a "Renewal Term") unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

**4.3 Rate Adjustment.** Upon renewal, the monthly fee may be adjusted by mutual written agreement. Any adjustment shall not exceed 10% of the prior term's rate without mutual consent.

## 5. DATA OWNERSHIP AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**5.1 Industry Partner Data.** The Industry Partner retains ownership of all Domain Data provided to the Technology Partner.

**5.2 Technology Partner IP.** Charlotte OS, including all substrate architecture, graph schemas, protocol engines, signal processing pipelines, and analytics methodologies, is and remains the exclusive intellectual property of the Technology Partner.

**5.3 Cross-Species Analytics.** Analytics derived from combining data across the Industry Partner's species divisions are joint insights. The graph structure and inference engine are Technology Partner IP. Both Parties may reference cross-species insights in their operations.

**5.4 Website and Brand Assets.** Design system, brand identity, and web presence materials created under this Agreement are Deliverables owned by the Industry Partner.

**5.5 Signal History.** The Signal History is jointly derived. Upon termination, the Industry Partner may request a structured export as described in Section 8.5.

**5.6 Aggregated Intelligence.** The Technology Partner may use anonymized and aggregated data to generate industry benchmarks. No individually identifiable seller, buyer, or sale-specific data will be disclosed without written consent.

**5.7 Improvements.** Improvements to Charlotte OS developed during this Agreement are the exclusive property of the Technology Partner.

## 8. TERMINATION

**8.1 Termination for Cause.** Sixty (60) days' written notice for material breach, with cure period.

**8.2 Termination for Convenience.** Ninety (90) days' notice + two (2) months' fee. No termination fee after Initial Term.

**8.3 Effect of Termination.** Standard wind-down, payment of accrued fees, return of confidential information.

**8.5 Data Export.** Within thirty (30) days: all Domain Data, all Deliverables, seller CRM data in CSV/JSON, and protocol definitions in human-readable format. Signal History and graph infrastructure are Technology Partner IP.
