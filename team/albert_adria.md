## Why Albert Adria

Every plate at Enigma is a five-sense problem. Taste + smell + touch (texture) + sight (plating) + sound (ambience) — completely atmospheric. That is what the OASIS is built to study. Albert Adria has been running nightly experiments in multi-sensory perception for 40 years, and the research lab he directed at El Bulli was the first culinary R&D facility in history.

He is not a metaphorical connection to the Taste pillar. He is the Taste pillar's most natural advisor on Earth.

## Career

### El Bulli (1985-2011) — 23 Years

Joined his brother Ferran at age 16. Rose from apprentice to head pastry chef to creative director. Co-established and directed **El Taller** (The Workshop) — the world's first dedicated culinary R&D laboratory — with Ferran Adria and Oriol Castro (1998).

El Bulli was named **The World's Best Restaurant five times** (2002, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009). Held **3 Michelin stars** throughout Albert's tenure. Closed July 30, 2011. Now the elBulli1846 museum/research center.

Techniques pioneered at El Bulli under Albert's direction:
- **Spherification** — liquid interiors with gel membranes
- **Espumas** — ingredient foams via nitrous oxide
- **Deconstruction** — isolating and recombining components of classical dishes
- **Aromatic courses** — presenting smell as an independent sensory input before the bite

### The elBarri Group (2011-2021) — Barcelona

After El Bulli closed, Albert built a constellation of restaurants in Barcelona's Paral-lel neighborhood:

## Enigma — The Five-Sense Laboratory

**Capacity:** 24 diners per service
**Duration:** 3-4 hours
**Courses:** ~40-45 dishes
**Design:** RCR Arquitectes (Pritzker Prize winners)

Seven themed rooms — La Cava, La Barra, La Planxa, formal dining, cocktail bar, kitchen counter, and more. Only three serve on any given evening. The menu changes every service. Servers present food without announcing what it is.

Albert has described it as **"entering a cathedral"** — a cathedral of food.

### The Five Senses at Enigma

| Sense | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| **Taste** | 40+ distinct taste experiences per evening. Same ingredient presented in sweet, savory, and bitter states simultaneously. |
| **Smell** | Aromatic elements prime the olfactory system before the first bite. Aroma as independent "course." |
| **Sight** | Plating as composition. Lighting, resin furniture, stainless steel mesh ceiling shift color/mood per room. |
| **Touch** | Foams that dissolve, spherifications that burst, gels that resist, temperature inversions. Tactile surprise. |
| **Sound** | Seven rooms = seven acoustic environments. Physical movement through space resets auditory baseline. |

**The meta-concept:** The five senses do not operate independently during a meal. They form a unified perceptual field. By controlling all five simultaneously — architecture, lighting, acoustics, service choreography, and food — the chef creates emotional states that transcend any single sensory channel.
