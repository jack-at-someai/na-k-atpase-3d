## The Cerebral Palsy Dimension

Cerebral palsy is a group of disorders affecting movement, muscle tone, and posture caused by damage to the developing brain. Matt has competed in natural bodybuilding at the national level with this condition — meaning he has built a physique that requires precise motor control using a neurological system that does not provide it natively.

This is not inspirational-poster material. This is signal processing research conducted on himself:
- **Motor signal degradation:** CP affects the signals from brain to muscle. Matt has spent decades learning to work around degraded motor pathways.
- **Proprioceptive compensation:** When your body's position-sensing signals are unreliable, you develop alternative strategies for spatial awareness.
- **Adaptation under constraint:** Building muscle symmetry with asymmetric neurological input requires constant calibration — adjusting form, load, and volume based on real-time feedback from a system with built-in noise.
