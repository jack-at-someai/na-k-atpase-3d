## Focus: Sensory Manipulation

Diego's research domain at SumAI is **all things sensory manipulation** — the systematic study of how the five senses can be isolated, recombined, enhanced, and deployed:

| Sense | Application in Culinary Science |
|-------|--------------------------------|
| **Taste** | Molecular deconstruction, flavor isolation, taste-memory triggers |
| **Smell** | Aromatic priming, olfactory-gustatory coupling (80% of "taste" is smell) |
| **Touch** | Texture engineering — foams, gels, spherifications, temperature contrasts |
| **Sight** | Plating as composition, environment design, visual expectation manipulation |
| **Sound** | Atmospheric design, acoustic environment as part of the dining narrative |

This is not limited to food. The principles of sensory manipulation — how to control perception across multiple channels simultaneously — apply to:
- **Medical environments** (how a hospital room's sensory design affects patient recovery)
- **Retail spaces** (how atmosphere drives purchasing behavior)
- **Marine environments** (how sensory cues drive animal behavior)
- **Industrial settings** (how multi-modal alerts improve safety response)
